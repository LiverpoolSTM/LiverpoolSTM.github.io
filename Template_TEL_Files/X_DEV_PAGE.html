Discussion board activities are held within Brightspace, and as such require an internet connection to access. If you are working offline, we recommend you participate in the activity wherever possible, and prepare your responses to copy and paste into discussions when you have internet access.

In discussion board activities, you will be asked to:

  • Read, view or otherwise engage with content
  • Discuss that content, and your thoughts on it, with your fellow students

If you are among the first students to contribute to any discussion board, we would like you to return later in the week to see what your peers have shared; and respond wherever you can contribute further to the discussion.

Further specific instructions will be included on the discussion board when you access it (see below the task for instructions).
