Introduction

Welcome to week 10 of the Global Health Practices module

As you move on with the programme, you will need to develop competencies for understanding research evidence, how the evidence is collected and critically appraising this. This week includes a brief introduction to the overall approaches to research, research paradigms and critical appraisal.

Learning objectives

By the end of this session you should:

  1. Have a critical understanding of research paradigm and methodologies
  2. Be able to review approaches suitable for addressing different questions in global health
  3. Develop skills for critical appraisal using CASP tools

Introduction

Welcome to weeks 11-12 of the Global Health Practices module - a 2 week learning consolidation block

We hope by the end of these 2 weeks you will have:

  1. A critical understanding of the summative assessment
  2. Completed writing the summative assessment
  3. Practiced submitting your assessment on Turnitin Practice submission point to check for similarity scores

Preparing for the consolidation weeks:

The aim of these two weeks is to allow you to reflect on your learning from the past weeks and its application onto practice – using the case study which is the basis of your summative assessment. To make the best use of this learning opportunity, you must read the assessment document thoroughly.

Do you have any questions regarding the summative assessment?

Post your questions about the assessment to this discussion board. Your tutors will collate the questions to form the basis of synchronous weekly seminar in week 11. Here are some things to consider which may help you:

  1. The good things I like about the assessment are... (up to 150 words)
  2. The questions I have about the assessment are...
